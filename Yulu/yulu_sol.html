Hypothesis testing now¶

One tailed test

$H_0:$ Count on weekday is equal to count on weekend

$H_1:$ Count on weekday greater equal to count on weekend

$H_0: \mu_1 = \mu_2$

$H_1: \mu_1 > \mu_2$
